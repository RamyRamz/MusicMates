About Us


What We Do

MusicMates Inc. is a not for profit organization designed to bring social interaction opportunities to people with social and special needs through the use of music.

Our director, Shera Lumsden, is a mother of a child with special needs, a certified Health Information Management Professional, and in her final year of a BAH Music and Psychology Degree at Queen’s University. In her 15 years of teaching music, Shera has seen the difference music makes in the emotional, physical and social development of children, youth and adults with special needs. She channeled her passion for music into developing a program to help her daughter and others like her.

PROGRAMS



Kids 12 & Under

MusicMates is pleased to offer music programming designed to encourage social skills and create friendships. Our programs offer children, youth and adults the opportunity to make friends, learn sign language, and practice important social skills all through the use of music!

Programs run for ten weeks beginning at the end of September, end of January, and end of April. Summer activities vary every year. In between sessions, MusicMates participants are invited to in some fun social events such as music jams and field trips.

Teens & Adults

Filled with music, special guests, crafts and musical activities what better way to spend your summer! Spend your afternoons chillaxing with MusicMates! MusicMates is excited to offer its first Teens & Adults afternoon program. This program will run from 12-4pm each day and will be sure to keep our participants singing a tune all week long! There’s lots of space to relax if needed, dance and play an instrument of your choosing!
